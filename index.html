About Us

Established in 1996, Omega Fish is a leading processor of Fresh and Frozen Nile Perch.

We are located on the pristine shores of Lake Victoria with one plant in Bukoba and one in Mwanza, Tanzania. With a production capacity of over 50T per day, the vicinity of the plant with the lake guarantees immediate and efficient processing on a daily basis and ensures the characteristic flavour and freshness of the products.

With over more than 500000T of fish exported over the past 25 years, we became a benchmark for quality fresh and frozen products obtained by processing the Nile Perch. Our main market is North America and Europe.

Our organisation is managed by educated professionals with experience of over 20 years in the industry.

Omega Fish's ultimate satisfaction is coming from clients and consumers happy to consume our product. Through impeccable process and delivery, we strive to deliver a product that our clients rave about and order over and over again.

The trust of our long term partners and clients is our most valuable asset and anchor in an ever-changing world.We look forward to develop new longlasting partnerships with serious players in the seafood market worldwide.

The Nile Perch

The Nile Perch is a fish from fresh water mainly available in the lake victoria.

It is considered by most to be the most tasty fish from fresh water and while it is available only in the Nile or the Lake Victoria, it is known and sought afters by the whole world.

Nile Perch fillets is filled with nutritive properties. It has the highest content of Omega-3 among fishes. 710 mg of Omega-3 fatty acids are the highest levels found in any Seafood.

Researchers have found that Omega- 3 fatty acids from seafood may reduce the risk of blood clotting, and also reduce levels of cholesterol. Also Omega-3 have positive effects on other disorders such as cancer, arthritis, asthma, and improve brain development and eyes tissues.
